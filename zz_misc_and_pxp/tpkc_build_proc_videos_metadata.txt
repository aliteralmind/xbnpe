Title:
XBNPE-TPKC: Build process (1 of ???): Preparation

Description:
First in a series demonstrating a full XBNPE-TPKC build process.

This video shows how to prepare for a completely new build, by eliminating the auto-generated files from the previous build.

This build is a "test" build, meaning only a subset of TextPad categories are processed. The purpose of a "test" build is for demonstration and testing of the TPKC build process.

There are fifteen build processes (actually sixteen, since 03 is actually two: 03 and 03a), split into four "master" builds: 1of4, 2of4, 3of4, 4of4. The first two master-builds (parts 1 and 2) are for TPKC-administrators only. Parts 3 and 4 are for TPKC-users.
