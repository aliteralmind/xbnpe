With the cursor in this text:

Goodbye
au revior
chao
fare-thee well[CURSOR]
we've had a lovely time

Typing
    /au/-skf
executes "skf" with "au" and "-" as its parameters. It results in this:

Goodbye
au revior
chao
fare-thee well[CURSOR]
we've had a lovely time

Further details and options are provided at the end of its execution.
